Overview

Bay Tree Cottage is a pretty, Grade II listed cottage situated in picturesque Sherborne Lane, one of the oldest streets in Lyme Regis. The shops, restaurants, cinema, pubs and the beach are only a short stroll away.

Recently refurbished and modernised, the cottage is comfortable and spacious with all modern conveniences. The accommodation comprises of and open plan sitting/dining room leading through to a modern kitchen. Stairs from dining area lead up to twin bedroom to front, double bedroom to back and a spacious modern shower room.
